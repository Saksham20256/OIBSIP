About Us

Any One Can Put The Heat To The Meat, But Only A Few Can Barbeque!! THE GRILL REAPER was founded in 2004 by one of our Promoters. We currently own and operate 23 outlets in India, 3 outlets in UAE, 1 outlet in Malaysia and 1 outlet in Oman. One of the leading casual dining chains in India, THE GRILL REAPER pioneered the concept of “over the table barbeque” live grills embedded in dining tables – allowing guests to grill their own barbecue’s right at their tables.
Indian cuisine is a natural part of the diet in the markets in which THE GRILL REAPER Restaurants operate. We continue to offer new menu options, both vegetarian and non-vegetarian, largely based on Indian cuisine and use seasonal customer preferences to introduce new dishes. We also run popular food festivals at our THE GRILL REAPER Restaurants, thereby offering our customers a range of Indian and international cuisines.
